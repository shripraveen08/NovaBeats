# Privacy Policy — NovaBeats

**Last updated:** 2024

---

## Summary

**NovaBeats collects absolutely no personal data. Ever.**

---

## What data we collect

**Nothing.**

NovaBeats does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. There are no:
- User accounts
- Analytics or tracking
- Crash reporting services
- Advertisement SDKs
- Third-party data collection tools

---

## What stays on your device

The following data is stored **only on your device** and never leaves it:

- Your music library and local file paths
- Your playlists and playlist names
- Your liked/favourited songs
- Your recently played history
- Your app settings and preferences
- Downloaded music files

This data is stored in the app's private storage on your Android device. It is never uploaded, synced to a server, or shared with anyone.

---

## Third-party services

NovaBeats connects to the following external services **only to fetch music content**:

### Jamendo (jamendo.com)
- Used to: Search and stream Creative Commons music
- Data sent: Your search queries and IP address (standard HTTP request)
- Their privacy policy: https://www.jamendo.com/legal/privacy-policy
- No personal account is created or required

### Internet Archive (archive.org)
- Used to: Search and stream public domain music
- Data sent: Your search queries and IP address (standard HTTP request)
- Their privacy policy: https://archive.org/about/terms.php
- No personal account is created or required

**Note:** Any app that connects to the internet sends your IP address as part of the standard HTTP protocol. This is unavoidable and is not unique to NovaBeats. Neither Jamendo nor Internet Archive are given any identifying information beyond what is technically required to serve content.

---

## Permissions used

| Permission | Why it's needed |
|-----------|----------------|
| `INTERNET` | To stream music from Jamendo and Internet Archive |
| `READ_MEDIA_AUDIO` | To read local music files on your device (Android 13+) |
| `READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE` | To read local music files on your device (Android 12 and below) |
| `FOREGROUND_SERVICE` | To keep music playing when the app is in the background |
| `WAKE_LOCK` | To prevent the phone from sleeping during playback |
| `POST_NOTIFICATIONS` | To show the playback notification and controls |
| `SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM` | For the sleep timer feature |

No permission is used for any purpose other than what is stated above.

---

## Children's privacy

NovaBeats does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children. Since we collect no data at all, there is no special risk to children's privacy.
